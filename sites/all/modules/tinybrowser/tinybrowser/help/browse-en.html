Using Quick Upload

At the Browse pane, you can upload multiple files without switching to the Upload tab by using Quick Upload feature. There are two ways to use Quick Upload feature.

1. Click and select
The first method is to clik the Quick Upload tab and select file(s) at the file open dialog which will be displayed.

2. Drag and drop
The second method is to simply drag and drop file(s) from your PC desktop to the Quick Upload tab.

In either case, uploading process immediately starts and you will see the progress indicator with a link to cancel uploading each file.

Context Menu Support

Browse pane supports context menu for quick image operations such as rotation, cropping, resizing, renaming, deleting and so on. Simply right click on the image/file will bring the context menu.
